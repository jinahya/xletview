14  package xjavax.tv.service.selection;
15  
16  /**
17   * <code>AlternativeContentEvent</code> is generated to indicate that
18   * "alternative" content is being presented during the presentation of
19   * a service. Alternative content is content not actually part of the
20   * requested service, such as content related to conditional access
21   * failures (e.g., purchase dialogs or advertising for content for
22   * which the user is not yet authorized). The presentation of
23   * alternative content is always initiated by the system and never by
24   * applications.<p>
25   * 
26   * This event will be generated in two situations:
27   * 
28   * <ul> <li> At the end of a successful service selection operation,
29   * this event will be generated if any of the service components being
30   * presented are alternative content. Under these conditions, the
31   * generation of this event signals a change in state of the service
32   * context from the <em>presentation pending</em> state to the
33   * <em>presenting</em> state.  A <code>NormalContentEvent</code> will
34   * not be generated.  </li>
35   * 
36   * <li>
37   * During the presentation of a service, this event will be generated
38   * if any of the service components being presented are replaced by
39   * alternative content.  One example of this is the expiration of a free
40   * preview period. In this case, generation of this event does not
41   * impact the service context state model.
42   * </li>
43   * </ul>
44   * 
45   * Presentation failures enforced via a conditional access system may
46   * be reported by this event or by a <code>SelectionFailedEvent</code>
47   * with the <code>CA_REFUSAL</code> reason code. Which of these is
48   * used depends on the precise nature of the conditional access
49   * system. Applications must allow for both modes of failure.
50   * <A HREF="../../../../javax/tv/service/selection/SelectionFailedEvent.html"><CODE>SelectionFailedEvent</CODE></A>, <A HREF="../../../../serialized-form.html#javax.tv.service.selection.AlternativeContentEvent">Serialized Form</A></DL>
51   * <HR>
52   * 
53   * @author Martin Sveden
54   * @statuscode 4
55   */
56  public class AlternativeContentEvent extends PresentationChangedEvent{
57  	/**
58  	 * Constructs the event.
59  	 * 
60  	 * @param source - The ServiceContext that generated the event.
61  	 */
62  	public AlternativeContentEvent( ServiceContext source){
63  		super(source);
64  	}
65  
66  }

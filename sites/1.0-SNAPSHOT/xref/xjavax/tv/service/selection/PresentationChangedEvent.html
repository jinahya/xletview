16  /**

21   * interaction with the CA system.  It is generated when neither
22   * <code>AlternativeContentEvent</code> nor
23   * <code>NormalContentEvent</code> are applicable.<p>
24   *

26   * querying the current <code>ServiceContentHandler</code> instances

29   * <A HREF="../../../../javax/tv/service/selection/ServiceContentHandler.html#getServiceContentLocators()"><CODE>ServiceContentHandler.getServiceContentLocators()</CODE></A>, <A HREF="../../../../serialized-form.html#javax.tv.service.selection.PresentationChangedEvent">Serialized Form</A></DL>

33   * @statuscode 4 
34   */
